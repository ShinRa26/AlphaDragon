At Gig Hub we believe there is so much great local music out there,
but it still remains a little too difficult to discover for our liking...

Gig hub aims to create an easy way for music lovers to quickly and easily look for local gigs, remember intersting bands and buy tickets directly.

The wall area acts as a great medium to effortlessly sample a bands media which we believe is a much better way to advertise than any poster or listing.

Users who sign up can save bands they like and remember them for another time, stay up to date with the next time they are playing, receive special offers and new media.
